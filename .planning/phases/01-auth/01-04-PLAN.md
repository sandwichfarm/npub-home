<objective>
Wire the auth system into the page: call `restoreSession()` on mount, conditionally render
the `LoginModal`, show the `OwnerBadge` when `isOwner` is true, and add the footer
Login / Logout link. Build `OwnerBadge.svelte` as the Phase 1 owner indicator placeholder.

This is the last plan in Phase 1. After this, the full auth flow is end-to-end: visitor sees
the page with a footer Login link; owner logs in, sees the management UI appear reactively,
refreshes and stays logged in.

Purpose: The auth singleton and modal components built in Plans 02-03 are useless until wired
into the page. This plan connects them and makes AUTH-05 (owner detection), AUTH-06 (session
persistence on reload), and AUTH-07 (logout trigger) observable to the user.

Output: Updated `+page.svelte` with full auth integration, new `OwnerBadge.svelte`.
</objective>
